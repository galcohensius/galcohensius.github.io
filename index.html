Gal Cohensius

Game Algorithm Developer & Problem Solver

I specialize in writing AI Players that play games.

My work includes developing agents for games like Spades, Gin Rummy, Contract Rummy, Domino, and Uno. I’ve also created innovative solutions, such as an algorithm to measure the difficulty of Klondike-Solitaire levels, and modular microservices like Elo rating systems and leaderboard services.

I’ve also used machine learning methods like deep neural networks (DNN) and random forests to predict how players behave, helping to better understand and enhance their gaming experience.

While I write most of my code in Java and Python, my focus is always on designing elegant, efficient algorithms that enhance gameplay and solve complex challenges.
